## About Me

I'm a final-year Ph.D. Candidate in the School of Computer Science at University of Nottingham, supervised by <a href="https://research.nottingham.edu.cn/en/persons/xiangjian-he/" style="text-decoration: none;">Prof. Xiangjian He</a>. Prior to that, I worked as a research assistant at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=oVG2zEkAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN" style="text-decoration: none;">Prof. Zhen Chen</a>. I received the M.S. degree from The University of Hong Kong in 2023, the B.S. degree from University of Lincoln in 2019. 


## Research Interests

Currently, my research interests primarily lie in **Medical Image Analysis**, **Image Segmentation**, and the application of **Foundation Models** in clinical diagnosis.
